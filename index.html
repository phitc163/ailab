About

The AI Lab, established by the Faculty of Information Technology at Ho Chi Minh City University of Education, serves as a hub for scientific research and innovation for faculty members and students within the department.

With a mission to advance knowledge and foster collaboration, the lab aims to contribute to the growth of Information Technology and Artificial Intelligence in Vietnam. By providing cutting-edge resources and a collaborative environment, the AI Lab supports groundbreaking research, inspires academic excellence, and promotes the integration of AI into diverse fields, aligning with the nationâ€™s technological and educational goals.
